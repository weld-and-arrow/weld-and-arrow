# The Weld and the Arrow — III. Proofs

*Third of three files: the anti-reduction argument in full — the part of the system addressed to the philosophers — closed by the pole-reducibility corollary, and followed by the disclaimers, enumerated. Cross-references to the companion files are marked (Theory) and (Theorems).*

## Why karma won't reduce

The principle leans on karma being irreducible to the impersonal field — to **inga alone**, Row 3's dependence-pole stripped of the weld (and so stripped of exactly the I-making). The question is entirely grid-internal: why doesn't karma reduce to the blind arrow without the agent-index? Here is why.

Karma is *act-of-an-agent* bearing *fruit-for-an-agent* — an agent-indexed designation with no substrate of its own, not the impersonal field wearing a veil. Because the agent has no base, karma cannot be *read off* that field: the reduction is not merely hard, it is **malformed** — it feeds an index-free input (the field of conditions) to an index-requiring designation (*whose* fruit?), and there is no agent-base from which to supply the index. The self it must locate is the I-making of the Attainment section (Theory) — the weld's for-me-ness (shu, Row 3), not a placement stated by Row 2 — and no field-fact can manufacture one, because I-making is an *act* done at act-time, and a field of conditions holds no act-time weld. The load-bearing premise is a **typing claim**, not a claim about the world's furniture: *nothing self-indexed is stored*. The field may carry as much as the tradition ever gave it — series, seeds, dispositions, delivery — all of it index-free; what it cannot carry is a mineness, because mineness is made, not had.

The identity claim — that this *is* karma, not merely a rendering of it — leans entirely here, so the continuum-reductionist has to be met in full, and he has a name: Siderits's reductionism about persons and karmic desert (*Personal Identity and Buddhist Philosophy*) is the strongest existing statement of the position called malformed here, and it is his chariot that must be taken first (*na ca so na ca añño*, the reaper neither the sower nor another). And he must be met as what he is: a **two-tier conventionalist**, not a locator of selves. Siderits never needed a real index — only a useful fiction, a convention that earns its keep — so no reply of the form *you cannot find the self* touches him; he wasn't looking. What the argument must show is narrower and harder: that the fiction's office — what karmic ownership is *for* in the tradition's hands — discharges where the convention cannot reach. That demonstration is the spine at this section's end; the typing argument first says why the tool mis-fits. The chariot has no own-being; "chariot" is a convention laid over a base — axle, wheels, pole, assembled — and *which* chariot is answered by pointing at that standing configuration under its name. No chariot-substance is found, and none is needed. The reductionist reads karma the same way: *whose* fruit is fixed by continuity-convention over a causal series, no self located, no self wanted. But mark *why* the move works — because a chariot is a **state-designation**, a configuration *had* over a base at a time; continuity-convention can track a state across a series precisely because at each moment there is a configuration standing there to be named.

Karmic ownership is not that. It is not *had* over a base but *made* — mine-making, the weld (shu) enacted at act-time; the "whose" is not a standing configuration to be named but a deed to be done, made by the act and re-made by the reception-act's reach-back, with nothing indexed stored between (not held, only re-welded). So there is no indexed base-at-a-time for continuity-convention to range over — only welds at act-times, and between them a field that carries conditions and no mineness. The reductionist is handed a **thing**-fixing tool (convention over a standing base) for an **act**-fixing job (an index that is made, not had). The tool is not weak; it is mis-typed — and *that* is what "malformed, not merely hard" comes to: the chariot can be fixed because the chariot is there at each moment to fix; karmic ownership cannot, because between welds nothing indexed is there, and the welds are acts the tool cannot reach.

### The five collapses

Five collapses must be blocked. **Verbal** — *you have only relabelled a series of momentary graspings, which the reductionist already grants.* But the reductionist grants graspings as *dharmas in the series* and then fixes ownership by continuity *over* them; the claim here is the stronger one, that ownership *is* the act-time weld, not a continuity-fact about a series of welds. Take the act away and no ownership is left to track — the continuity-fact is parasitic on welds it does not itself supply. And the parasitism-claim is not assumption but origination: convention can redistribute a mineness some act has enacted — string it, relabel it — but a report cannot *originate* a doing; strip the welds of their token-reflexive content and continuity strings bare occurrences with nothing proprietary in them to track. The disagreement is real, and only the weld survives the between-deeds gap where nothing indexed is stored. **Soul** — *an index made each act and stored nowhere looks like an author re-appearing to sign each deed.* The reverse: a soul is precisely what *would* be stored — a standing base persisting between deeds to own the series. The weld is *emptier* than the reductionist's continuum, not fuller, since it declines even the standing configuration the reductionist keeps; MMK 8 leaves no doer behind the deed, so the weld is the whole of the authoring and lapses when the act lapses — and the reach-back is no exception, spent at its own reception-time, a retrospective soul declined along with the forward-facing kind. **Process** — *a designation need not range over a standing base: 'the same dance', 'the same flame' are fixed by convention over causally connected events, nothing danced or burnt between the phases — so fix ownership the same way, over the weld-events themselves.* But such conventions work by conferring something external to each event — being a phase of the dance is nothing a step does to itself; the step is indifferent to which dance it belongs to — while what a weld makes is token-reflexive: the index points at the very act that makes it, *this act's agent*, as 'now' points at its own utterance, and is spent at its own act-time. A relation over welds inherits their causal connections, not their self-reference — it can string welds like beads, but *mine* is not a bead; it is the stringing each weld does to itself, once. Converting a series of this-act-for-this-act indices into a standing *for-the-same-one* is precisely the manufacture of a bearer MMK 8 forecloses. So the flame is a fair model of the series — inga, the arrow, the seeds, granted in full; seeds are flame-phases par excellence, causally connected, nothing in one phase *for* that phase — and no model of ownership; the counter conflates the series-question (hard, and answered by conditions) with the index-question (malformed). And if *mine* is re-read as the shareable predicate *belongs-to-continuum-C*, the circle closes: C is individuated by its welds, so the definition routes through the very index it was to replace — only the act-time weld breaks the circle. **Diachronic** — *the circle argument cuts both ways: your loop must also be individuated, and if by causal connectedness among welds, sameness-of-being over time is a series-fact and the diachronic whose-question is reclaimed.* Granted and disarmed: the loop *is* individuated by inga — that is a series-fact, ceded from the start — but individuation of the series was never the ownership-question. The diachronic *whose* decomposes without remainder into the delivery-fact (field) and the reach-back (weld, made fresh at reception); what the reclaim would need is a third, standing diachronic ownership-fact between the two, and the tradition itself declines it — *neither the sower nor another*. The reductionist reclaiming the diachronic reclaims what was already his; the index, synchronic at sowing and synchronic-with-reach-back at reaping, was never on the table.

**Supervenience** — the last collapse concedes the four and ascends a level. *Every weld-occurrence is itself a field-describable event — you say so yourself: receptions re-pitch the configuration, and the re-pitch is an inga-fact. So take the totality of delivery-facts plus weld-occurrence-facts: third-personal throughout, and it fixes everything — no two worlds could agree on all of it and differ on who owns what. Ownership supervenes; whatever the weld adds beyond its occurrence is a mode of presentation, and reductions owe facts, not glosses.* The answer is one typing, and it borrows no apparatus: **supervenience is a relation among fact-sets, and the weld was never submitting a fact.** The occurrence-fact (*an appropriation happened at t*) supervenes fine and was ceded to the field from the opening page; the appropriat*ing* is a doing, and a doing is not a fact-candidate — the fact *that* it occurred is, and is granted. Everything the objector's totality contains is granted, and the totality is complete — complete over facts; what it omits is nothing factual, and "omits" is already the wrong verb. So *malformed* receives its final cash-value: **feeding an act to a fact-relation**.

### Austin credited as register, declined as mechanism

The classical register for a saying that does rather than reports is Austin's, and the debt is acknowledged exactly where it exists: the act/constative split has his name on it. But Austin is credited as register and **declined as mechanism**, and the declining is load-bearing in its own right. Austinian performatives are moves within conventional practices — roles distributed in advance, an authorized performer standing among the felicity conditions, uptake in the offing — and a promise without a promiser is not an infelicitous promise; it is no promise at all. The weld could never have been that: it is the very act that *makes* the agent, so no prior performer could figure among its conditions, and importing the apparatus would dig a performer-shaped hole at the system's deepest joint. What carries the mechanism is what carried it before the vocabulary arrived: **MMK 8 plus token-reflexivity**. Doer and deed co-arise, neither prior, neither based; the weld's index is self-anchoring — *this act's agent* is constituted by the act, not presupposed by it, exactly as "now" needs no prior time-owner in order to refer. No practice, no role, no uptake: **the weld is not a speech act**, and the performer-side condition never had jurisdiction here. "Confers," accordingly, is demoted to display — legal over the pattern as "mirror" is, illegal as a mechanism of social force — and what stands where the force-vocabulary stood is the determination clause (Theory: Attainment): the arrogation is an actual driving in the token, a doing with a causal composition Row 2 reads, which is all the categorical ground the appropriating ever needed.

The supporting move is the de se, enlisted for exactly one thing — agency — and no more: Lewis's two gods know every third-personal fact and still cannot do anything, lacking not information but location, and Perry's sugar-spilling shopper shows what changes when *mine* lands — on Perry's own account **no new worldly fact**, only the reflexive mode under which action becomes possible. That is deliberately this side of metaphysics: the de se secures that the field cannot act — the two gods: carrying everything, doing nothing — at act-time, where karma's verdicts fire and the question is not *what is the case* but *what is to be done, by this one, now*; it is not asked to secure a remainder over the facts, because the load-bearing move denies there is a remainder of that kind to secure.

Promising is thereby demoted from model to **analogy**, with the analogy's one load stated and owned: promising resembles the weld in being a doing that no constative rendering exhausts, and the grid still types promissory *standing* as field-face (a conditioning-fact about the series, third-personal, what others may hold the promiser to) and the *binding* as enacted — but that split is now this paper's claim about promising, made in act-hood currency, not a credential inherited from a theory of speech acts. Whether it is defensible for promising simpliciter or quietly revisionist is left open here; for karma it is not optional — it is the doctrine, since nothing indexed is stored.

### The hostage, relocated and priced

Then the hostage — relocated once more, and priced. The reply's fate rides on **act-hood**, not on speech-act theory, and the difference is what the post-Austin literature makes vivid. The deflationary program that renders explicit performatives truth-apt — "I promise" as self-verifying assertion or declaration (Searle; Bach and Harnish) — dissolves *speech-act* performativity, and passes this argument by: rendering "I promise" truth-apt does not render the *promising* a fact; it relocates where the doing sits. The position that would reach the weld is stronger and rarer — whoever renders everything *done* exhaustively as facts *about* doings reduces this weld along with every doing there is — a metaphysic considerably more expensive than mainstream speech-act theory, and that is the dispute the grid enlists in. Skeptics of the de se (Cappelen and Dever, *The Inessential Indexical*) strip the supporting move and leave the load-bearing one standing; the banner's *within the lens* carries the act/fact typing, and the argument is offered, not compelled, to one who declines it.

### The offices-spine

And this section's honesty-clause is paid where it bites. Thinned to act-hood, the metaphysical resistance is exactly as wide as *acts are not facts* — which the reductionist can grant while insisting that every *fact* the soteriology needs supervenes, threatening the dispute with the verbal collapse this section's own first entry preempts. What closes it is not more metaphysics but the object's usage, and this is the identity-claim's **spine**, not a flourish: every office karmic ownership holds in the tradition discharges at act-time — cetanā as the measure of the deed (AN 6.63), reception itself a deed, practice a weld, remorse and absolution verdicts on welds (Cunda — Theorems: Three killings), and dedication a weld with the routing left to the field (pariṇāmanā — Theorems) — while the diachronic bookkeeping is precisely where the tradition deflates itself (*na ca so na ca añño*, the reaper neither the sower nor another). Relabel the continuity-fact "ownership" and the word is kept while the office is lost: nothing the tradition does with karmic ownership consults a cross-gap fact, and everything it does consults the act. So Siderits keeps every fact and the fiction too; what the fiction cannot be said to do is karma's work, because karma's work, in the object's own hands, is act-time work throughout. *Malformed* says the state-tool cannot reach the act; the spine says the office never sat where the tool reaches. The identity-claim is argued from the object's usage, not stipulated against it.

### Contemporary placement

Two contemporary neighbours should be placed, since the vocabulary overlaps theirs. Ganeri's account of appropriation (*The Self* — ahaṃkāra and upādāna worked into a first-personal stance) is the nearest ally, and differs in exactly one load-bearing respect: his appropriative activity is a standing structure of the person; this weld is spent, per-act, stored nowhere — the difference the five collapses defend. Zahavi's constitutive for-me-ness is the more instructive case, and it receives the taxonomy's fourth verdict (Theorems) rather than a cell: a **retype**. His for-me-ness is thin — pre-reflective givenness, explicitly *not* appropriative I-making, a distinction he defends at length against no-self readings — so classifying it as clench would import ahaṃkāra into a notion built to exclude it. And the grid cannot spare the notion: the weld's own token-reflexivity *is* a thin act-time for-me-ness — the deed lit as this-deed-for-this-doer — which is Zahavi's phenomenon pared of exactly one predicate: **standing**. The genuine disagreement is that single predicate — mineness as a structural feature of consciousness as such versus mineness enacted and spent — and only the standing, held as the being's floor-structure, freezes (what occupies the clench-as-structure cell proper is the Sartrean face: anguish as the very form of consciousness). That a live phenomenological position forces the generator's rarest verdict — redrawing a distinction's content rather than landing in a cell — is the generator behaving as advertised, run for once against the contemporary literature rather than the classical; the verdict's other exercise is internal — the determination clause forcing open the grid's own disposition/act cell (Theory: Attainment) — so the retype is a working part, not a one-off.

### The claim in its earned form

So the strength the opening claims is earned right here, and stated in its earned form: **the index-question is answered without remainder** — karma is not the impersonal field plus a convention, because the convention has nothing indexed to range over between welds, and the welds are acts it cannot reach. Karma *is* the welds — act-indexed, empty, re-enacted, closing their own loop at each reception — riding a field that carries everything diachronic and owns none of it. Within the emptiness-under-erasure lens that is karma with no remainder. Whether it is *simpliciter* "exactly what karma is" turns on forcing the state/act typing on someone standing outside the lens — which the theory compels *inside* it (nothing indexed is stored, so there is no indexed state to convention over) and offers, but cannot compel, without. Hence the honest banner: the index-question without remainder **within the lens**.

This does not over-generate among impersonal designations — emptiness is universal, so *generic* no-own-being can't be the licence, or every reduction would collapse; the verdict fires *only where a designation's answer must route through a self*. Impersonal conditioning among dharmas — dependent arising without a doer — lets convention fix an answer without locating a self, so it stays determinate and merely hard. But the verdict does generalize along one axis, and the generalization is embraced rather than blocked: any designation whose index is *made by an act, token-reflexive, and spent at act-time* resists the state-tool the same way — promising has the structure (the analogy above), and first-personal desert generally may. That is a scoped anti-reductionism about **enacted indices**, not about persons wholesale: every series-question — psychology, continuity, prediction, the whole Parfitian and Sideritsian machinery of connectedness — stays with the field, untouched. The opposition to Siderits is therefore exact and narrow: not that persons fail to reduce, but that *ownership-at-act-time* was never a person-fact of the reducible kind — an act mis-filed as a state. If the argument proves that much about promising too, that is a result, not a leak — and a result now claimed in this paper's own currency, act-hood, rather than inherited from anyone's theory of speech.

A supplement, not load-bearing: the grid's own taxonomy can be turned on the objection. *Is the reaper really the same being as the sower?* — asked as a standing-fact question — is itself a freeze, a state-tool applied to an act-job, identity held as a floor-claim. Under act-time diagnosis it separates into two well-formed questions (*did the deed condition this fruit?* — delivery, inga; *is this fruit welded mine now?* — reach-back, shu), and the residue fuses away. But this diagnosis is offered after the substantive decomposition, never instead of it: the moral-coherence worry the question carries — *why this being, for that deed?* — deserved the MMK 17 answers (Theorems), not a verdict on the questioner.

### Pole-reducibility: the corollary owned

The argument carries a corollary at the summit, and it is stated as entailed rather than tolerated: **reduction succeeds exactly where welding ceases.** At share-zero no self-pole index is made and no reach-back appropriates (the transposition's theorem-half — Theorems); so the terminus is the one place the state-tool fits — nothing act-indexed there for it to miss, the buddha/stone difference carried by the function/share split alone, and both halves field-describable, since function and share are drive-composition through and through. The cleanest constructive witness is the adaptive device at zero effectiveness (Theory: the domain joint): chariot-typed furniture, terminus-typed, zero output — the typing rode on composition alone, with no weld anywhere to resist. That the fiercest anti-reductionism about ownership coexists with the summit's total reducibility is not a tension tolerated but an entailment stated: the resistance's whole ground was the weld, so where welding has ceased the resistance was never supposed to survive. The reductionist is right about the buddha — and right for the grid's reasons, which is the *malformed* verdict's scope-clause honoured at its far end: the verdict fires only where a designation's answer must route through a self, and at the pole none does.

### Two guards and the verdict's own tier

Two guards hold the line. The agent resists reduction by being *more* thoroughly empty, not by being a special substance — else a soul is re-smuggled, forward-facing or retrospective. And it is not a Moorean non-natural fact: the reduction is malformed *because its base is empty* — a dissolutionist reading on a self-emptying floor, not the positing of an extra fact.

**Malformed is a conventional-tier verdict, not a floor-claim.** At the floor there is no agent and no fruit-for-anyone; MMK 17 deflates the karma–fruit relation there, and a reduction speaking only at the floor would find nothing to reduce and nothing to preserve. The verdict fires at act-time, where "whose fruit?" is live and must route through the weld-index. So the tiers behave by a rule: **they separate under act-time diagnosis and fuse at the floor (and at genjō — the share-zero pole, no arrogation left).** That rule is what makes *malformed* well-formed — a claim about the conventional tier that dissolves, harmlessly, below. The same rule dissolves the fox (not-fall at the floor, not-obscure conventional), governs the two-middles distinction (genjō provisional, shō non-attaining), and applies to the *malformed* verdict itself: each separates under act-time diagnosis and fuses beneath it.

Nor does dropping to the floor save the reduction: there it succeeds by default over nothing — no fruit, no *whose*, no reduction left with an object. The continuity-convention was always a conventional-tier tool offered for a conventional-tier fact, so the verdict fires precisely where the reduction speaks and fuses precisely where it falls silent. Reducer and weld share a tier or share nothing.

## The disclaimers, enumerated

The following are conventional and my own — original moves, each flagged against the canonical position it departs from or extends.

1. **The tiering, the separate/fuse rule, kannō-sōe, and the coined compound shugenjō** — contra Dōgen, who keeps all ultimate, and who fused the very shushō I have re-tiered (shō at the floor, shu conventional); and contra the *late* Dōgen doubly, since Jinshin inga repudiates the tiered fox-reading this paper keeps, a disagreement owned in Theory rather than footnoted, with the act-time gloss on the doubling (Daishugyō diagnosing, Jinshin inga instructing) offered as my own reading and not as a dissolution of the contra; and contra the Mujō seppō fascicle (and Zhanran on 無情有性) a third time, the insentient preaching deflated to the object-axis.
2. **Shō's agency as *lent*** — necessarily agential because never unwelded, yet not agential of itself.
3. **For-me-ness located in the weld** — shu, Row 3's enactment pole, the I-making (ahaṃkāra) — so that self is a dependently-arisen *process* the act makes rather than a state the grade holds.
4. **The reception-weld's reach-back** — upādāna typed as the two-place token-reflexive index by which loop-closure is enacted at each reception, "the sower reaps" split into a report-face (true simpliciter, field) and an ownership-face (done, full or vacuous) and never a standing relation — my own typing, though the word is canonical.
5. **The three-register fact-sorting** — field-facts carried, weld-facts made and spent, Row 2 stated — a sorting of registers, not of kinds of fact — and with it the refined premise *nothing self-indexed is stored*, under which the tradition's seeds are absorbed deflated as index-free conditioning-facts rather than deleted (an engagement with, not a reconstruction of, the Yogācāra machinery Chan presupposed).
6. **The reading of Linji's 無位真人** along a three-way seam (無位 = shō, the floor-face / 真人…出入 = shu, the acting / 無位真人 = the weld, shushō-ittō).

## Glossary

*Supplements the Theory and Theorems glossaries; terms defined there are not repeated.*

- **The five collapses** — the replies the anti-reduction argument must block: **verbal** (mere relabelling), **soul** (the index as stored author), **process** (ownership by convention over events, the flame and the dance), **diachronic** (ownership reclaimed as a series-fact), **supervenience** (ownership fixed by the totality of third-personal facts).
- **State-designation vs. act-fixing** — the chariot is *had* over a standing base at a time, so convention can track it; karmic ownership is *made* at act-time, so there is no indexed base for convention to range over; handing the state-tool the act-job is what *malformed* comes to.
- **Continuity-convention** — the reductionist's instrument: *whose* fixed by convention over a causal series; it can redistribute a mineness some act enacted, never originate a doing.
- **Two-tier conventionalist** — Siderits's actual position: no real index sought, only a useful fiction; met by relocating the fiction's office, not by hunting the self.
- **The offices-spine** — the identity-claim argued from the object's usage: every office karmic ownership holds in the tradition (cetanā, reception, practice, remorse and absolution, dedication) discharges at act-time; the diachronic bookkeeping is where the tradition deflates itself.
- **The act/fact typing** — a doing is not a fact-candidate; the occurrence-fact (*that* it happened) is ceded to the field, the doing is not; supervenience is a relation among fact-sets, so *malformed*'s final cash-value is *feeding an act to a fact-relation*.
- **Austin credited, declined** — the act/constative split acknowledged as register; the mechanism refused: no practice, no role, no uptake, the weld not a speech act — the performer-side felicity condition has no jurisdiction because the index is self-anchoring.
- **"Confers," demoted** — conferral-talk kept as display only; the arrogation's categorical ground is the actual driving the determination clause reads.
- **The two gods / the de se** — Perry and Lewis enlisted for agency only: the field as the two gods (everything carried, nothing done); the shopper's *mine* adding no worldly fact; supporting, never load-bearing.
- **The promising analogy** — promising demoted from model to analogy; the standing/binding split owned as this paper's claim in act-hood currency — possibly revisionist for promising, doctrinal for karma.
- **The hostage** — what the reply's fate rides on: act-hood, not speech-act theory; constative renderings of performatives (Searle; Bach–Harnish) relocate the doing without factizing it and pass the argument by; only an exhaustive rendering of doings as facts-about-doings would reach the weld; Cappelen–Dever strip the support, not the load.
- **Thin for-me-ness** — Zahavi's pre-reflective givenness, explicitly non-appropriative; recognized as what the weld's token-reflexivity itself needs — the deed lit as this-deed-for-this-doer — pared of one predicate: *standing*; the disagreement narrowed to that predicate alone.
- **The Sartrean occupant** — what fills the clench-as-structure cell after the Zahavi retype: anguish as the very form of consciousness.
- **The sower-reaps split** — the report-face (true simpliciter, a field-fact) versus the ownership-face (done at reception, full or vacuous); never a standing relation between the two.
- **The pole-reducibility corollary** — reduction succeeds exactly where welding ceases: at share-zero nothing act-indexed remains for the state-tool to miss; the reductionist is right about the buddha, for the grid's reasons.
- **The two guards** — the agent resists reduction by being *more* thoroughly empty, never a special substance; and *malformed* is no Moorean non-natural fact — the reduction fails *because its base is empty*.
- **The verdict's own tier** — *malformed* is a conventional-tier verdict, not a floor-claim: it fires at act-time where "whose fruit?" is live and fuses harmlessly below; reducer and weld share a tier or share nothing.
- **The disclaimers** — the enumerated original moves, each flagged as conventional and the author's own against the canonical position it departs from or extends.
